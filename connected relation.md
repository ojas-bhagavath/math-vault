A [relation](relation.md) $R$ on a set $A$ is said to be connected if  
for every pair of $x$, $y$ in $A$ such that $x\neq y$, either $(x,y)$ or $(y,x)$ belong $R$.  
That is, for any pair of $x$, $y$ in $A$, either $x=y$, or $xRy$, or $yRx$.
