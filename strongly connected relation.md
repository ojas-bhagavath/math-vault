A [relation](relation.md) $R$ on a set $A$ is said to be strongly connected if it is [connected](connected%20relation.md) and [reflexive](reflexive%20relation.md).  
That is, for any pair of $x$, $y$ in $A$, either $xRy$ or $yRx$.
